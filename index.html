About Us

Khudgharz is Pakistan pop-rock music band. It started off during Covid_19 days in June 2020 from a roadside tea shop in Karachi. During a very short period of time, the band gathered huge fan following not only in Pakistan but from all over the world where people can understand Urdu and Hindi. Currently, Khudgharz is the most followed Pakistani music band on Youtube with over 2.2 million subscribers. The band announced the debut album by the title of Hum Khudgharz on September 18, 2023. The title track of the album released on September 23rd, 2023.
